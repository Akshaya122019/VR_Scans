1.5 TMR Scan

At VRScan, we offer 1.5 TMR Scan using cutting-edge technology to provide detailed and accurate imaging of internal organs, tissues, and structures. Whether for neurological, orthopedic, or full-body scans, our state-of-the-art MRI system ensures high-resolution images for precise diagnosis and treatment planning. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) is a state-of-the-art, noninvasive technology that provides detailed visualization of the body's organs, tissues, and skeletal system. At VR Scan, our MRI unit captures high-precision images of any body part from multiple angles and planes with exceptional clarity. Our advanced MRI techniques are completely safe, utilizing no ionizing radiation and posing no biological hazards, ensuring a risk-free imaging experience Our non-invasive MRI scan provides high-resolution isotropic 3D images with exceptional soft-tissue contrast. VR Scan is the trusted choice for doctors and patients, offering clear, detailed, and dynamic imaging of the human body for accurate diagnosis. Our team of experts are committed to providing you with accurate and reliable results as quickly as possible.

Why Choose Our 1.5 TMR Scan?

Choosing an 1.5 TMR Scan can offer several benefits depending on your medical needs. Here are some reasons why you might consider opting for an 1.5 TMR Scan:

Higher Resolution Images

1.5 TMR Scan machines often provide higher resolution images, which can help in detecting smaller or more subtle abnormalities that might be missed by standard MRI machines.

1.5 Tesla Available

Our 1.5 Tesla delivers ultra-high-resolution imaging for enhanced clarity and precision. With twice the magnetic strength of standard MRI, it provides faster scans and sharper details.

Non-Invasive and Safe:

MRI scans do not use ionizing radiation, making them a safer option compared to CT scans or X-rays, especially for patients who require multiple imaging sessions.

OUR MRI SERVICES

We offer comprehensive MRI services, including brain, spine, musculoskeletal, cardiac, and full-body scans. Our advanced imaging technology ensures high-resolution, accurate diagnostics for various medical conditions. Experience safe, fast, and precise MRI scans with expert analysis.

Brain MRI

A brain MRI provides detailed images of the brain's structure, helping diagnose conditions like tumors, strokes, or neurological disorders

Spine MRI

A spine MRI offers detailed images of the spinal cord, vertebrae, and surrounding tissues, aiding in diagnosing issues like herniated discs, spinal stenosis, or injuries.

Epilepsy MRI

A musculoskeletal MRI provides detailed images of bones, joints, muscles, and soft tissues, helping diagnose injuries, arthritis, or sports-related conditions.

Joint MRI

A cardiac MRI provides detailed, non-invasive imaging of the heart's structure, function, and blood flow, aiding in the diagnosis and management of heart conditions.

Angiogram MRI

MRI Angiogram is a safe, non-invasive imaging test that provides highly detailed images of blood vessels using magnetic fields and radio waves.

Spectroscopy MRI

It helps detect abnormalities at a molecular level, providing crucial insights into brain disorders, tumors, metabolic diseases, and more.

Stroke Protocol MRI

A Stroke Protocol MRI is a specialized imaging test designed to detect, evaluate, and manage stroke-related conditions quickly and accurately.

Cardiac MRI

A Cardiac MRI is an advanced imaging test that provides detailed, high-resolution images of the heart and blood vessels.
