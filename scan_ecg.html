Why should you take an ECHO/ECG/TMT ?

  • Heart health is crucial for overall well-being, and diagnostic tests like ECHO, ECG, and TMT.

  • Regular heart health assessments can save lives by detecting issues early and guiding timely treatment.

  • ECHO, ECG, and TMT are essential diagnostic tests that provide valuable insights into your heart's condition.

  • TMT are powerful tools to evaluate and monitor your heart's function.

Benefits of Taking ECHO, ECG, and TMT

Identifies heart issues before they become severe. Helps reduce the risk of heart attacks or strokes. Guides doctors in providing personalized care and recommendations. Confirms heart health or highlights areas that need attention. Regular heart screenings can save lives. Consult your doctor to determine which test is right for you based on your symptoms and medical history. an ECHO, ECG, or TMT today and take a proactive step towards a healthier future. TMT evaluates how your heart performs during physical activity. People with chest discomfort, suspected coronary artery disease, or high-risk cardiac profiles. Athletes or individuals planning high-intensity physical activities.

TYPES OF ECG TESTS

Resting ECG

Performed while the patient is lying still. Electrodes are placed on the chest, arms, and legs.

Stress ECG

Conducted while walking or running on a treadmill to monitor heart response.

Event Monitor ECG

Used for extended heart rhythm tracking when symptoms occur intermittently.
